A spinlock is a low-level synchronization mechanism used in multithreading to 
protect a critical section.Instead of putting a thread to sleep when the lock is busy, 
the thread keeps looping (spinning) until the lock becomes available.

How a Spinlock Works :
A thread tries to acquire the lock.
If the lock is free, the thread acquires it and enters the critical section.
If the lock is busy, the thread keeps checking repeatedly (busy waiting).
Once the lock is released, one spinning thread acquires it.
